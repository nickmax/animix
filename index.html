About Us

Animix is an animal health and nutrition company that specializes in provision of solutions and services geared towards enhancement of animal health and nutrition. We partner with reputable international companies that guarantee safety, efficacy, quality, reliability and performance of their products all the time. The company manufactures a diverse range of premixes for the animal feed industry in the region. Our range of products include feed additives, premixes, veterinary medicines and acaricides.

Our mission is to improve the health and performance of animals by providing innovative and natural solutions.

Our vision is to improve the quality of life for animals by supporting adequate and healthy food through proper nutrition of animals. We strive to set the standard for quality, safety and value in the development and supply of innovative products.
